# PsySense AI - AI Evaluation Disclaimer

Last updated: May 5, 2026

## Short Disclaimer

Use this version in dashboards, reports, and interview screens:

```text
PsySense AI provides structured interview insights for recruiter review. AI-generated scores, summaries, match results, and proctoring signals are decision-support information only. Final hiring decisions must be made by human reviewers.
```

## Report Disclaimer

Use this version in PDF reports:

```text
This report contains AI-assisted interview analysis generated by PsySense AI. Scores, summaries, recommendations, and proctoring indicators are intended to support recruiter review and should not be treated as final hiring decisions. Human reviewers should consider the candidate's full application, interview context, accommodations, role requirements, and any technical issues before making employment-related decisions.
```

## Candidate-Facing Disclaimer

Use this version before the interview begins:

```text
This interview may be transcribed and analyzed using AI-assisted tools. PsySense may generate structured scores and collect integrity signals such as tab switching, fullscreen exits, copy/paste attempts, and multiple-face detection. These outputs are reviewed by recruiters and are not final hiring decisions by themselves.
```

## Recruiter-Facing Disclaimer

Use this version in the recruiter dashboard:

```text
AI scores and proctoring risk indicators are review aids. They may be affected by answer quality, audio clarity, resume context, browser behavior, network conditions, camera quality, lighting, accessibility needs, or model limitations. Review the underlying answers and context before deciding.
```

## Why This Matters

AI hiring tools can affect candidate trust, legal exposure, and customer confidence. PsySense should be positioned as a structured review system, not an autonomous hiring authority.

The correct product promise is:

> PsySense helps recruiters evaluate candidates faster and more consistently.

The product should not claim:

> PsySense makes final hiring decisions automatically.

## Recommended Product Rule

Every place that shows a final score, recommendation, rank, or proctoring risk should include a visible or accessible reminder that the output is advisory and requires human review.

## Known Limitations

AI-generated outputs may be affected by:

- Audio quality.
- Accent, speech style, or language fluency.
- Network interruptions.
- Candidate device quality.
- Browser behavior.
- Resume formatting.
- Incomplete job descriptions.
- Model limitations.
- Prompt or scoring rubric limitations.
- Camera angle, lighting, or accessibility needs.

Recruiters should review full context before making decisions.
