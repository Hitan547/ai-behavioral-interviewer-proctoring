# PsySense AI - Terms of Use

Last updated: May 5, 2026

## Overview

These Terms of Use describe the acceptable use of PsySense AI, an AI-powered behavioral interview and candidate screening platform.

This document is a pilot-ready draft and should be reviewed by legal counsel before public commercial launch.

## Product Purpose

PsySense is designed to help recruiters and hiring teams conduct structured candidate screening. It may provide:

- Resume and job description matching.
- AI-generated interview questions.
- Candidate interview transcription.
- AI-assisted answer scoring.
- Proctoring and integrity signals.
- Recruiter dashboards and reports.

PsySense is a decision-support tool. It does not replace human judgment.

## Human Review Requirement

Recruiters and hiring organizations are responsible for all final hiring decisions.

AI-generated scores, candidate rankings, match results, proctoring risk indicators, and report summaries must be reviewed by humans before any employment-related decision is made.

Users must not use PsySense as the sole basis for:

- Hiring or rejection.
- Compensation decisions.
- Promotion decisions.
- Disciplinary actions.
- Any legally significant employment decision.

## Recruiter Responsibilities

Recruiters and customer organizations are responsible for:

- Providing accurate job descriptions.
- Using the platform lawfully and fairly.
- Informing candidates that AI-assisted evaluation and proctoring signals may be used.
- Obtaining any required candidate consent.
- Reviewing AI outputs for accuracy, fairness, and context.
- Avoiding discriminatory or unlawful use of the platform.
- Maintaining confidentiality of candidate data and reports.

## Candidate Responsibilities

Candidates are responsible for:

- Providing accurate account and interview information.
- Completing interviews honestly.
- Not attempting to manipulate, bypass, or disrupt the interview or proctoring flow.
- Using the platform only for the intended interview process.

## AI Output Limitations

AI outputs may be incomplete, incorrect, biased, or affected by audio quality, network conditions, resume formatting, interview context, or model limitations.

Users should treat AI outputs as structured signals requiring human review, not as final truth.

## Proctoring Limitations

Proctoring and engagement indicators are contextual signals. They are not automatic proof of cheating or misconduct.

Examples:

- A tab switch may occur because of a system notification.
- A fullscreen exit may happen due to browser behavior.
- Multi-face detection may be affected by camera reflections, family members, or background movement.
- Engagement detection may be affected by lighting, camera angle, device quality, or accessibility needs.

Recruiters must review proctoring events with context before taking action.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not:

- Upload unlawful, malicious, or irrelevant content.
- Attempt unauthorized access to accounts, systems, data, or infrastructure.
- Reverse engineer or abuse the platform.
- Use the system for discriminatory or unlawful screening.
- Share candidate data with unauthorized parties.
- Misrepresent AI-generated outputs as final human decisions.

## Data and Confidentiality

Candidate data, resumes, interview reports, and proctoring records should be treated as confidential recruiting records.

Customer organizations are responsible for controlling access to recruiter accounts and exported reports.

## Availability

For pilot deployments, PsySense may be provided on a best-effort basis. Availability, uptime, backups, and support commitments should be defined separately for paid production customers.

## Payment and Subscriptions

If billing is enabled, subscriptions, quotas, plan limits, renewals, cancellations, and refunds should follow the customer agreement and payment provider terms.

Billing demo mode must be disabled before public production launch.

## Suspension or Termination

Access may be suspended or terminated for:

- Security risk.
- Unauthorized use.
- Payment failure.
- Violation of these terms.
- Customer request.
- End of pilot or subscription.

## Changes to Terms

These terms may be updated as the product and commercial model evolve.

## Contact

Questions about these terms should be directed to the PsySense product owner or the organization administering the deployment.
